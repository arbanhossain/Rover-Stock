Team ICARUS

Perseverance Reimagined

Team Icarus aims to create immersive VR applications that will mesmerize the players by providing unparalleled experiences

Our mission is to push the boundaries of virtual reality by combining cutting-edge technology with innovative design to transport users to new worlds

Experience our game in WebVR

3D Gallery

User interactive repository containing Perseverance and essential components of it along with exhibition of previous candidate landing sites such as Jezero Crater, NE Syrtis, and Columbia Hills.
Orbital Transfer

Captivating and delicate orbital manuever from earth’s orbit to mars’s orbit with user control that guides the spacecraft towards the Red Planet.

Mars Exploration

Detailed adventure of Perseverance on Mars including its scientific mission, drilling into rocks, and exploring the terrain. Being a part of the mission, tiny ingenuity shows its powered flight hovering over the martian landscape.

Our Impacts and Goals

Through this project, we aim to not just provide an experience, but create lasting impact that will resonate for years to come. Here are some of our goals and future impact:

1. Educational Innovation
2. Visual Learning
3. Awareness of Technical and Space Milestones
4. Utilize innumerable open NASA data to create a once-in-a-lifetime experience
5. Sparking Curiosity for Next Generation of our country’s youth
6. Underprivileged children getting affordable quality education
7. Filling the void in VR space experience
8. Suitable for children with special needs
